The website was created to inform users about four (4) different areas in which Technology have played a vital part in the transformation of crime.
These areas are crimes against individuals, crimes against property, crimes against businesses and crimes against government.

Each area contains:

i. at least three (3) examples of crimes in the past with the absence of technology
ii. corresponding examples for (i) in which the use of technology has evolved the way that particular crime is committed
iii. Actions/responses that may mitigate the effect of (ii)
iv. At least three specific crimes that may be mitigated with the use of technology
